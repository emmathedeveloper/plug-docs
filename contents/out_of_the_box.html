Out of The Box

There are pre-made widgets in Plug, so you don't go through the stress of coding out a Bare widget. Let's see them.

üìù

There isn't a widget for every HTMLElement YET, just the essentials. Therefore, you shouldn't discard the usage of a Bare widget. Especially if you are going for something custom.
